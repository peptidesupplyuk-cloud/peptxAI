Overview

Synthetic GHRH analog that stimulates steady growth hormone release from the pituitary gland. Increases IGF-1 levels naturally.

How it works

CJC-1295 (no DAC) is a synthetic analog of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). It binds to GHRH receptors on the pituitary gland, stimulating the release of growth hormone (GH). This increase in GH subsequently elevates insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1) levels, which are involved in growth and metabolism.

What the research describes

  • Increases growth hormone levels
  • Elevates IGF-1 levels
  • Enhances fat metabolism
  • Promotes muscle growth
  • Improves recovery times

Evidence summary

There are limited human trials on CJC-1295 (no DAC). Some small studies suggest it can increase GH and IGF-1 levels, but larger, well-controlled trials are needed to confirm these effects and assess safety.

Evidence grade C — Early research.
