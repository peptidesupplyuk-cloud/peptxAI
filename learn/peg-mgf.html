Overview

PEGylated version of MGF with extended half-life. Provides systemic rather than local muscle repair effects over several hours.

How it works

PEG-MGF is a PEGylated form of Mechano Growth Factor, which is a splice variant of IGF-1. It works by activating satellite cells in muscle tissue, promoting muscle repair and growth. The PEGylation extends its half-life, allowing for systemic distribution and prolonged activity compared to the non-PEGylated form.

What the research describes

  • Enhanced muscle repair
  • Increased muscle hypertrophy
  • Improved recovery from muscle damage
  • Potential increase in muscle strength
  • Systemic distribution of growth signals

Use areas

PEG-MGF is researched across multiple areas:

Fitness

PEG-MGF is relevant for enhancing muscle repair and growth, potentially benefiting athletic performance and recovery.

Health optimisation

May support muscle health and recovery, but lacks human data for general health optimisation.

Evidence summary

There are no human clinical trials specifically evaluating PEG-MGF. Most evidence comes from animal studies and in vitro experiments showing enhanced muscle repair and growth. Human data is lacking, and more research is needed to confirm efficacy and safety in humans.

Evidence grade C — Early research.
