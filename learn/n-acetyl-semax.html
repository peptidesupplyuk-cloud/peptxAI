Overview

Enhanced version of Semax with improved metabolic stability. Stronger BDNF upregulation and longer-lasting cognitive effects.

How it works

N-Acetyl Semax is a synthetic peptide that enhances brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF) expression, which supports neuronal survival and growth. It modulates the activity of the melanocortin receptors, influencing neuroplasticity and cognitive function. The acetylation improves its metabolic stability, allowing for prolonged activity in the central nervous system.

What the research describes

  • Increased BDNF levels
  • Enhanced cognitive function
  • Improved memory retention
  • Reduced anxiety
  • Neuroprotection

Evidence summary

There are limited human trials specifically on N-Acetyl Semax. Existing studies primarily focus on its parent compound, Semax, which has shown cognitive and neuroprotective benefits. More research is needed to confirm these effects in humans for the acetylated version.

Evidence grade C, Early research.
