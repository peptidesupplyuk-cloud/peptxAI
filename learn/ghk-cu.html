Overview

Naturally occurring copper-binding peptide with regenerative properties. Stimulates collagen synthesis and tissue remodelling.

How it works

GHK-Cu binds to copper ions, facilitating their transport into cells. It activates tissue remodeling by upregulating collagen synthesis and promoting wound healing. The peptide also modulates gene expression related to inflammation and oxidative stress, enhancing skin repair and regeneration.

What the research describes

  • Enhances wound healing
  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Improves skin elasticity
  • Promotes hair growth

Evidence summary

There are a few small human trials that suggest GHK-Cu can improve skin healing and reduce signs of aging. However, larger and more rigorous studies are needed to confirm these effects and establish optimal dosing.

Evidence grade B — Emerging.
