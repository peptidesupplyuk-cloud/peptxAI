Overview

Signal peptide that stimulates collagen I, III, and IV production. One of the most clinically validated cosmetic peptides for anti-aging skincare.

How it works

Palmitoyl Pentapeptide-4 works by mimicking the natural peptides involved in the skin's healing process. It stimulates the production of collagen types I, III, and IV, which are crucial for maintaining skin structure and elasticity. This peptide penetrates the skin barrier and signals fibroblasts to increase collagen synthesis, leading to improved skin firmness and reduced appearance of wrinkles.

What the research describes

  • Increases collagen production
  • Reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Improves skin elasticity
  • Enhances skin firmness
  • Promotes skin repair and regeneration

Evidence summary

There are several human clinical trials that demonstrate the efficacy of Palmitoyl Pentapeptide-4 in reducing wrinkles and improving skin texture. These studies generally show positive outcomes, but more large-scale, long-term studies are needed to fully understand its effects and safety profile.
