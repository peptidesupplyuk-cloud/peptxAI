Overview

Oral GH secretagogue that mimics ghrelin to increase GH and IGF-1 levels. Not a peptide but commonly grouped with them. Significantly increases appetite.

How it works

MK-677 works by mimicking the hormone ghrelin, binding to the ghrelin receptor in the brain. This stimulates the release of growth hormone (GH) from the pituitary gland. The increase in GH subsequently raises levels of insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1), which plays a role in growth and metabolism.

What the research describes

  • Increases growth hormone and IGF-1 levels
  • Enhances muscle mass and strength
  • Improves bone density
  • Increases appetite
  • Improves sleep quality

Use areas

MK-677 is researched across multiple areas:

Longevity

MK-677 may support longevity by maintaining muscle mass and bone density in older adults.

Fitness

MK-677 is relevant for athletes seeking to enhance muscle growth and recovery.

Health optimisation

MK-677 can be used to improve body composition and metabolic health.

Evidence summary

Several human trials have shown that MK-677 effectively increases GH and IGF-1 levels, with associated improvements in muscle mass and bone density. However, long-term safety data is limited, and more research is needed to fully understand its effects.

Evidence grade B — Emerging.
