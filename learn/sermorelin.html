Overview

Shortened form of GHRH that stimulates natural growth hormone production. Previously FDA-approved for GH deficiency diagnosis.

How it works

Sermorelin is a synthetic peptide that mimics the action of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). It binds to the GHRH receptor on pituitary cells, stimulating the release of endogenous growth hormone. This process enhances the natural pulsatile secretion of growth hormone, which is crucial for growth and metabolism.

What the research describes

  • Increases endogenous growth hormone levels
  • Improves body composition by reducing fat mass
  • Enhances muscle mass and strength
  • Supports bone density
  • Improves sleep quality

Use areas

Evidence summary

There are several human trials that have demonstrated sermorelin's ability to increase growth hormone levels and improve body composition. However, most studies are small and short-term, with limited data on long-term safety and efficacy.

Evidence grade B, Emerging.
