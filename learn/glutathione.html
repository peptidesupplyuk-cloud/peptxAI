Overview

Master antioxidant produced by every cell. SubQ form bypasses poor oral bioavailability. Supports detoxification, immune function, and skin brightening.

How it works

Glutathione acts as a powerful antioxidant by neutralizing free radicals and reactive oxygen species, thereby protecting cells from oxidative stress. It also plays a crucial role in detoxification processes by conjugating with toxins to make them more water-soluble for excretion. Additionally, glutathione is involved in regenerating other antioxidants like vitamin C and E, and supports immune function by maintaining the redox state of cells.

Glutathione supports overall health by enhancing detoxification, reducing oxidative stress, and supporting immune function.

Evidence summary

Several human trials have investigated glutathione's effects, showing benefits in reducing oxidative stress and improving skin health. However, the evidence is mixed regarding its efficacy in immune support and detoxification, with some studies showing limited effects. More large-scale, controlled trials are needed to confirm these benefits.

Evidence grade B, Emerging.
