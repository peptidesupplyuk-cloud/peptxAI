Overview

Nootropic peptide that enhances focus, memory, and cognitive function. Approved in Russia for cognitive and neurological conditions.

How it works

Semax is a synthetic peptide that primarily acts as a neuroprotective agent by modulating the expression of brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF) and other neurotrophins. It enhances the availability of neurotransmitters like dopamine and serotonin, which are crucial for cognitive processes. Semax also inhibits the breakdown of enkephalins, which are peptides that regulate pain and stress responses. Additionally, it may improve cerebral blood flow, contributing to its cognitive-enhancing effects.

What the research describes

  • Improves memory retention and recall
  • Enhances attention and focus
  • Reduces symptoms of anxiety and depression
  • Promotes neuroprotection and recovery after brain injury
  • Increases levels of BDNF in the brain

Use areas

Semax is researched across multiple areas:

Longevity

Semax may support brain health and cognitive function, which are important for healthy ageing.

Health optimisation

Semax may enhance cognitive performance and mood, contributing to overall mental well-being.

Evidence summary

There are several human trials conducted in Russia that suggest Semax improves cognitive function and mood, particularly in patients with neurological conditions. However, these studies are limited in number and often lack rigorous controls. More high-quality, placebo-controlled trials are needed to confirm these effects.

Evidence grade B, Emerging.
