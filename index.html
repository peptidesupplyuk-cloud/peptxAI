Questions

Frequently asked

What is PeptX.AI?

PeptX.AI is a research-intelligence platform for peptides, supplements and biohacking. It distils published research into evidence-graded cards, answers your questions with a grounded AI, lets you compare compounds, and (soon) helps you interpret your own bloods and DNA. Everything is an educational summary of published research — for laboratory and research use only.

Is this medical advice?

No. PeptX.AI describes what published research reports; it does not recommend, prescribe, or instruct you to use any compound, dose or protocol, and it is not a substitute for a qualified healthcare professional. All compounds are referenced strictly for laboratory and research use only.

Where does the data come from?

Each compound is built from published scientific literature and pharmacokinetic data, graded A–C by evidence strength, and summarised in plain English. We show the basis for each rating and keep expanding the base with citations and outcome data.

How is the AI kept accurate and safe?

The assistant is grounded in our own knowledge base, trained on the most common questions, and constrained to describe research rather than prescribe. It's rate-limited per user, has a safety filter for harmful requests, and always carries a research-use disclaimer.

Do I need an account?

No — browsing the compound index, comparing and asking the AI are open. An account unlocks the personal tools: saving compounds, the health assessment, and private blood/DNA analysis.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes. PeptX.AI is built mobile-first and works as a web app today, with a native iOS app on the roadmap. The card deck is designed to be swiped like a real deck.
